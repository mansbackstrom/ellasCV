01 - ABOUT

Profile

I'm an architecture student with a deep passion for urban development — how cities grow, adapt, and make room for people. My focus lies in designing parks, neighborhoods, and public spaces that work within tight, complex urban environments like those found in Göteborg.

Through studio projects, design competitions, and coursework in sustainable urbanism, I'm building the skills to bridge architectural vision with real-world city-building. I thrive on the challenge of turning constrained sites into vibrant, livable places.
